Chili

Description

This is an easy chili recipe great for the cold winter months. its packed with nutrition and perfect for meal prep.

Ingredients

  • 1 meduim onion
  • 1 large bell pepper (any color)
  • 1 796ml can of crushed tomatos
  • 1 156ml can of tomato paste
  • 500g ground beef
  • 2 cans of your choice of beans (i like red kidney and brown beans) or 240g (raw weight) of cooked dried beans
  • 30g of cooking oil
  • 15g brown sugar
  • 45g white vinegar

spice blend

  • 5g extra hot chili powder
  • 10g smoked paprika
  • 8g cumin
  • 3g garlic powder
  • 3g onion powder
  • 1.5g oregino
  • 7g salt
    •

    Steps

    1. mix the spice blend together then finely dice the onion and bell pepper
    2. in a skillet add the oil and brown the ground beef then add the onions and peppers until softend
    3. into a slow cooker add both tomato products and add the ground beef, onions and peppers along with all of the spice blend
    4. simmer on high for 4-6 hour until meat turns very soft and the flavours have developed
    5. add the beans and sugar simmer for 1 more hour on a low heat
    6. finish will the vinegar and serve with your favorite topping
