Dinner Rolls

Description

This recipe shows how to make simple dinner rolls in under 90 minutes they are very similar to a japanese milk bread meaning they have a very fluffy interier and rich flavour. they are easy to make and can be topped with a garlic & herb butter if desired.

Ingredients

  • 360g flour
  • 125g water
  • 125g milk
  • 15g sugar
  • 15g butter
  • 4g yeast
  • 6g salt

Steps

  1. start by combining every ingredient except the flour and mix until well combined
  2. combine flour and knead dough ball for 6-8 minutes or until the dough is smooth and a bit tacky avoid adding flour unless absolutely necessary
  3. shape dough into a round ball and let proof for 30 minutes
  4. divide dough into 8 peaices shape into round balls and place them in a well oiled skillet or loaf/baking pan
  5. let proof for 20 minutes brush on melted butter or a melted compound butter if desried
  6. bake at 350 for 20-25 minutes or until internal tempature reaches 180-200 degrees F
