Creme Brulee

Description

This is by far the easiest fancy desert you can make its rich and creamy with a lovely crunchy carmel candy top.

ingredient

  • 2 egg yolks
  • 30g sugar
  • 10g vanilla extract
  • 175-200g heavy cream (enough to make 1 cup total liquid)
  • plus 20-30g sugar for the candy top which will be used at the end
    •

    Steps

    1. mix 30g sugar with the egg yolks beat until well combined
    2. add vanilla and heavy cream to egg mixture and stir until homogenous
    3. pour mixture evenly into 2 standard 4 ounce ramekins
    4. bake at 250 degrees F for about 45 minutes or until internal temp reaches 180 degrees F
